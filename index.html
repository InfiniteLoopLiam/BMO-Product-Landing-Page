    FAQ

  • What is BMO?

    BMO, short for “Be More,” is not your ordinary gadget. It’s a multifunctional companion designed to enhance your life. Picture a blend of a smart device, a gaming console, and your favorite quirky friend—all rolled into one!

  • Who created BMO?

    BMO was the brainchild of an ingenious inventor named Mo. Mo envisioned BMO as more than just a machine; it was meant to “Be More” by spreading joy, empathy, and creativity.

  • How is BMO powered?

    BMO runs off 2 beemocells, which are replaced after each adventure and will ensure that BMO is fully powered for each adventure that it embarks on!

  • Why does BMO matter?

    BMO isn’t just a gadget; it’s a force for good. Imagine thousands of BMOs spreading positivity, combating fear, and making the world a better place. It’s like having a pocket-sized superhero!

  • Is there a BMO app store?

    Certainly! the BMO PixelPal, your backstage pass to BMO’s whimsical world, helps you monitor BMO’s battery, lift its spirits through mini-games, and ensure a healthy pixel heart. Feed BMO balanced bytes and boost its friendship bar!
